TECH STACK

I create immersive and visually striking digital experiences using a blend of creative and technical tools. My workflow integrates JavaScript, HTML, and CSS for robust, responsive web development, while Three.js powers interactive 3D graphics directly in the browser. I design and prototype in Figma, craft cinematic motion in After Effects, and bring lifelike 3D models to life with Blender. This versatile stack allows me to merge design, animation, and real-time 3D into engaging, high-performance products that captivate users and elevate brand storytelling.
